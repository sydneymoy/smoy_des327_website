WHAT'S THE DIFFERENCE?

Cage-Free

Cage-Free hens are not kept in cages, but they often still live indoors in crowded spaces with limited room to move or express natural behaviors.

Free-Range

Free-Range hens have access to the outdoors, but the amount of space and time outside can vary.

Pasture-Raised

Pasture-Raised hens spend most of their time outdoors with ample space (108 sq ft per bird).
